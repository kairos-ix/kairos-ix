## About

I'm a Diploma IT student in Vadodara, Gujarat, India, building toward Application
Security. I'm early — Phase 0/1 of my own roadmap — and documenting the process in
public rather than waiting until I "know enough" to start.

Day to day, I build web apps (React, Node, Express, MongoDB, Tailwind) for small
businesses and hackathon teams. Alongside that, I'm working through networking,
Linux, and security fundamentals — hands-on, not just theory.

## What I've actually done so far

- Practical labs: WiFi handshake capture and deauth (aircrack-ng), ARP poisoning /
  MITM on Kali Linux — see `wifi-dos-attack`
- Working through TryHackMe, PortSwigger, and DVWA
- Lead developer for Team Kairos — built and submitted hackathon projects across
  fintech, civic-tech, and clinical data domains
- Built and shipped full websites for real clients and hackathon teams
- Keeping a public roadmap of what I'm learning and why — see `cybersec-roadmap`

## Currently

Working through Phase 1 of my roadmap: networking, Linux, Python, security
fundamentals.
